ABOUT ASUP FPOG CHAPTER

The Academic Staff Union of Polytechnics (ASUP), Federal Polytechnic of Oil and Gas (FPOG), Bonny Chapter was formally admitted into the National body on the 9th of December, 2021. Before then, the Chapter was on observer status at both the Zonal and National levels. The Chapter held her maiden Elections of Executives on the 25th of February, 2022.
