Comr. Akpuh Davidson C.

Welfare Secretary

Akpuh Davidson C. Holds a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical engineering from Rivers State University, and a master's degree Mechanical engineering Management from Rivers State University. He is currently a lecturer and also the HOD of Welding and Fabrication Engineering Technology department in Federal Polytechnic of Oil And Gas Bonny. Also he was a pump, Compressors, Engines maintenance personnel in SPDC, 2015-2018. Supervisor in Oropet Oil & Gas Nig. LTD. 2019- 2021, where he supervised the construction, installation and operation of a compressor gas station in Platform Petroleum company in Delta State. He is also a professional painter and an expert in all types of wall finishing designs. He was then elected as the Welfare Secretary of the Academic Staff Union Of Polytechnics (ASUP), Federal Polytechnic of Oil and Gas, Bonny Chapter. By virtue of his position, he now serves as the Pioneer Welfare Secretary of the Academic Staff Union of Polytechnics (ASUP) in the Chapter Executive Council (CEC).
