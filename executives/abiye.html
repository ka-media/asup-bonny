Abiye Aseminaso, MNSE, holds a Bachelorâ€™s degree in Chemical and Environmental Engineering from the University of Nottingham,UK and a Masters degree in Oil and Gas Engineering from the Robert Gordon University, Aberdeen, also in the UK. He is a cooperate member of the Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE), Nigerian Society of Chemical Engineers (NSChE), Institute of Chemical Engineers (iChemE), Nigerian Institution of Environmental Engineers (NIEE), Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE), among other professional organizations. He is a Pioneer Lecturer in the Federal Polytechnic of Oil and Gas, Bonny, where he was appointed the Pioneer Head of Department for Chemical Engineering Technology from 2015-2018. He was then appointed the Pioneer Dean, School of Engineering Technology from 2018-2021. In his time as Dean, he also served as the Acting Rector of the Polytechnic on 3 different occasions. In 2022, He was then Elected as the Pioneer Chairman of the Academic Staff Union of Polytechnics (ASUP), Federal Polytechnic of Oil and Gas, Bonny Chapter. By virtue of his position as Chairman of the Chapter Executive Council (CEC), he is now a member of the Zonal Executive Council (ZEC) and the National Executive Council (NEC) of the Union.
