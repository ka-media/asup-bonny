Comr. Tanko Mansur

Internal Auditor ASUP FPOG Chapter

Engr. Tanko Mansur is from the School of Engineering to be precised Building/ Civil Engineering Technology. A registered Engineer with the Council for the Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN) and a full-time participant of AGM in the Nigeria Society of Engineers (NSE).

He attended Kano State University of Science and Technology (KUST) Wudil to study Civil Engineering (2012). He was an Auditor I; Chairman Building Committee; and Financial Secretary for MCAN/NDLEA Jigawa State Command respectively (NYSC-2013). He had his postgraduate program at Bayero University Kano in M.Eng Water Resources and Environmental Engineering (2017).

A lecturer, a coordinator and supervisor of students' projects in the Department of Industrial Safety and Environmental Engineering Technology.

He is currently a pioneer Internal Auditor of ASUP FPOG-Bonny Chapter.
