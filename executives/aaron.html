Comr. Aaron Kiani T.

Secretary General

Mr. Kiani Aaron T. is a lecturer of Federal Polytechnic of Oil and Gas, Bonny. He is currently the Coordinator of Languages Unit of the School of General Studies (FPOG). He is the pioneer General Secretary of the Academic Staff Union of Polytechnics (ASUP), Federal Polytechnic of Oil and Gas, Bonny.

He obtained his first and second degree in Linguistics and Communication Studies at the University of Port Harcourt. His research interests include writing descriptive grammars, language & culture documentation and the tone-syntax interface. He has published articles in notable journals.

Currently, he teaches courses on Use of English and Communication in English at the School of General Studies.
