Comr. Nwachukwu Precious I.

Publicity Secretary

He holds Bsc in Management from Rivers State University of Science and Technology, Rivers State.

Msc in Human resources management from Ignatius Ajuru University of Education, Rivers State.

PhD in Human resources Management (in view) Ignatius Ajuru University of Education Rivers State.

Lecturer in the School of Business Studies and Management Technology, Federal Polytechnic of Oil and Gas Bonny.

He is the exams and record officer in School of Business Studies and Management Technology from 2018 to date.

Coordinator of both Human Resources Management department and Petroleum Marketing and Business Studies department from 2021 to date.

Coordinator of SIWES in School of Business Studies and Management Technology.

And member of several committees in the department of Petroleum Marketing and Business Studies.

In 2022 he was elected as the pioneer PRO of academic union of polytechnic , FPOG chapter and was appointed as the chairman of of research and journals committee for the Union.

He is an academic researcher who have successfully published twenty one(21) article in More seventeen (17)international journal and conferences.
